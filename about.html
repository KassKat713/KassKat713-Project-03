My Inspiration

I try and find inspiration from the music I listen to, such as goth music. An offshoot of the punk music scene, goth music embrasses love for the weird, the dark, and the emotional. The same can be said of ballet, an art form that transforms emotions and words into beautiful movement. Painting also provides a creative outlet for expressing my thoughts and feelings.

My Life Lessons

  • I have dyslexia and asking for help has always been so hard for me, I don't want someone to think I'm dumb. Learning to ask for help has been a game changer for me, I have found that people are more than willing to help me succeed, I just have to ask!
  • Failure is a part of the learning process and it's important to embrace it and learn from it. I have found that when I fail, I learn more than when I succeed. It's important to not be afraid of failure and to use it as a learning opportunity.
  • Trying new things is important because you never know what you might discover about yourself or the world around you if you don't step out of your comfort zone. I have found that when I try new things, I learn more about myself and the world around me. It's important to not be afraid of trying new things and to embrace the unknown.

Fashion and Technology

Fashion and technology are two of my biggest passions and I want to combine them in my career. I want to use technology to improve the fashion industry. I also want to use fashion to express myself and to inspire others. I believe that fashion and technology can work together to help people express themselves in new and exciting ways.
